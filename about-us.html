About Us

KittyCode Creative is an East Sussex based Web Design Agency established in 2024. While we are based in Eastbourne, we can work with clients anywhere in the world. No matter who you are, or where you are, we will always strive to offer the best possible service.

KittyCode Creative is ran by Austin Spillman, a creative and passionate multi-disciplined digital designer & web developer. With over 7 years of experience in the design and tech industry, we can guarantee that your website is in good hands.

Why Choose Us?

We pride ourselves on our client-centric approach. We take the time to understand your unique needs and goals, ensuring we deliver a website that truly reflects your vision. Our affordable pricing, coupled with our commitment to quality and exceptional service, sets us apart in the web design industry. We believe in building lasting relationships with our clients through trust, reliability, and outstanding results.

Our Mission

At KittyCode Creative, our mission is to provide high-quality, professional web design services to individuals and small local businesses. We are committed to helping our clients establish a strong online presence with websites that are both aesthetically pleasing and highly functional.
