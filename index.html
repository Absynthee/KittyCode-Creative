Eastbourne Web Design

KittyCode Creative is a brand-new digital creative agency based in Eastbourne, East Sussex. We specialise in creating beautiful and professional bespoke web design, front-end web development and design solutions.

What We Do

We offer a unique and personal service to each and every client. All of our websites are built from scratch and are available in monthly packages to keep your costs down.

Our web design solutions include an SEO service which helps your website rank well in search engines. If your aim is to raise visibility and gain traction on your website, we can help.

As well as web design and development, we provide a logo design service that is sure to improve your brand image.

Fully Responsive

Your website will fit all mobile screens sizes, tablets, and desktop sizes so new clients can access your site from anywhere.

Unique Design

Your website will be 100% handcrafted and totally unique to you. No templates here.

Accessibility

Making the web accessible benefits individuals, businesses, and society. Your website will be fully perceivable, operable, understandable and robust.

Why Choose Us

The cats behind KittyCode Creative strive to offer excellent, friendly personal service. No job is too big or too small. Feel free to contact us with any request, and we will try our best to achieve your vision.

Our goal is to provide unique and accessible websites for everyone. With our low pricing entry, we cater towards individuals promoting their personal website or portfolio, and small businesses hoping to improve their digital footprint.

FAQs

Why should we choose you?

We're certainly glad you're considering us! As a new agency we don't have the portfolio of others that have been around for years. However, we have all the skills and know-how to deliver you the best website for your needs... and we do it cheaper than anyone else.

I already have a website, I just need you to manage it for me. Is this something you offer?

Absolutely! We have no set pricing for this sort of request, so it will depend on a case-by-case basis. Send us a message and we will gladly manage your website for you.

Is hosting included in your packages?

Yes, all of our monthly packages include hosting charges. This enables us to keep your website up and running. If you stop your monthly payments before your contract expires, your hosting will also stop. If you're not on a monthly package, we can host for you for an additional fee.

Do you offer SEO?

All of our web packages include SEO. While we cannot guarantee that your website will be listed within the top search results of Google, we will do our best to get your website as close to the top as possible.
