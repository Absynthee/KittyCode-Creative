Announcing the Launch of a New Digital Web Design Agency

05 August 2024

web design agency
business

We are excited to announce the launch of a new digital web design agency based in Eastbourne, East Sussex, dedicated to delivering bespoke web design, front-end development, and creative solutions tailored to your needs.

Our Vision and Mission

KittyCode Creative was founded with a vision to create beautiful, functional, and accessible websites that stand out in the digital landscape. The mission is to empower businesses of all sizes by providing customised web solutions that drive growth, enhance online presence, and improve user experience.

What We Offer

We specialise in bespoke web design - every project is approached with a focus on individuality and uniqueness. Each website is crafted from scratch, ensuring it aligns perfectly with your brand identity and objectives. The services include:

  • Custom Web Design: Creating visually stunning and user-friendly websites that capture the essence of your brand.
  • Front-End Development: Implementing responsive and interactive web features that provide an optimal user experience across all devices.
  • SEO Services: Enhancing your website's visibility on search engines to attract more visitors and increase engagement.
  • Logo Design: Developing memorable and professional logos that strengthen your brand image.

Commitment to Accessibility and Sustainability

Inclusivity and accessibility are among our principles. Ensuring that all websites are usable by people of all abilities is a top priority. By following best practices in web accessibility, the aim is to make the internet a more inclusive space.

Additionally, sustainability is a key focus. Hand-coded websites are designed to be energy-efficient, reducing server usage and load times. This not only improves performance but also minimizes environmental impact, contributing to a greener digital environment.
