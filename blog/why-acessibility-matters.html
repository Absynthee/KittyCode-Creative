Why Accessibility Matters in Web Design: A Personal Perspective from KittyCode Creative

02 August 2024

At KittyCode Creative, we’re passionate about making the web a place where everyone feels welcome and included. As a digital web design agency, we’re committed to creating websites that are not only visually stunning but also accessible to all. Here’s why accessibility in web design is so important to us and how it can benefit your business.

Imagine visiting a website and struggling to read the text, navigate the menus, or interact with the content. For many people with disabilities, this is a daily reality. We believe that everyone, regardless of their abilities, should have equal access to information and services online. By incorporating features like alt text for images, keyboard navigation, and readable fonts, we ensure that our websites are accessible to everyone, providing a seamless experience for all users.

Legal Compliance

In the UK, the Equality Act 2010 requires businesses to make reasonable adjustments to ensure that people with disabilities are not at a disadvantage. Making your website accessible helps you comply with these legal requirements, safeguarding your business from potential legal issues. But beyond that, it’s about doing the right thing and fostering an inclusive environment for all.

Enhanced User Experience

When we design with accessibility in mind, we create a better experience for everyone. Clear navigation, well-structured content, and easy-to-use interfaces benefit all users, not just those with disabilities. Prioritising accessibility helps us enhance the overall user experience, making it easier for visitors to find information, interact with your site, and achieve their goals.

Reaching a Broader Audience

By making your website accessible, you’re opening your doors to a wider audience. Did you know that around 15% of the global population lives with some form of disability? By ensuring your website is usable for this demographic, you tap into a significant and often overlooked market, increasing your potential customer base and showing that your business cares about inclusivity.

SEO Benefits

Accessibility and SEO often go hand in hand. Many practices that improve accessibility, such as using proper heading structures and descriptive alt texts, also boost your site’s search engine optimisation. This means that accessible websites are often more likely to rank higher in search engine results, driving more organic traffic to your site and increasing your visibility online.

Social Responsibility and Brand Image

When you commit to accessibility, you’re showing that your business values inclusivity and social responsibility. This can enhance your brand image, foster goodwill, and build loyalty among customers who appreciate your dedication to making the web a better place for everyone.

Our Approach at KittyCode Creative

At KittyCode Creative, we weave accessibility into every stage of our web design process. From the initial planning phases to the final testing, we ensure that our designs are inclusive from the ground up. We also conduct thorough testing with assistive technologies, such as screen readers, to ensure our sites are navigable for all users. And we’re always staying updated with the latest accessibility guidelines, continually improving our practices to meet evolving standards.

Conclusion

Accessibility in web design is more than just a legal requirement; it’s a reflection of our values and a business advantage. By ensuring your website is accessible, you can reach a broader audience, improve user experience, and demonstrate social responsibility. At KittyCode Creative, we’re passionate about creating websites that are not only beautiful and functional but also accessible to all.

If you’re interested in making your website more accessible or starting a new project with accessibility in mind, we’d love to hear from you. Together, we can create a web that works for everyone.
