## Why Offer Light and Dark Themes?

While it might seem like just a design choice, allowing users to toggle between themes offers real benefits. A dark theme can reduce eye strain, especially in low-light environments, making those late-night browsing sessions far more comfortable. Alternatively, the light theme ensures readability during the day or in bright settings.

Beyond aesthetics, offering a theme switch improves accessibility. Users with certain visual impairments often benefit from adjustable contrast, allowing them to interact more comfortably with the site.

As well as that, when it comes down to user preferences it's better to have more people be able to enjoy your website without their eyes being blinded by the pure white background.

## Why We Made the Switch

As dark modes become increasingly popular across devices, we felt it was essential to provide this option for our visitors. Whether you're a fan of the dark side or prefer the brightness of a light theme, we've ensured that your experience on our website is as comfortable and engaging as possible.

Why it took so long? With more colours and code being added, there are more variables and more testing is required. However, it's absolutely the investment.

## Making Your Browsing Experience More Personal

Our goal is to give you control over how you engage with our content. Personal preferences matter, and whether you like your screens bright or dim, we've got you covered. So, try out the new toggle, and see which theme works best for you!
