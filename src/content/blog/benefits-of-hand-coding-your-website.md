## Performance and Speed

One of the most noticeable benefits of hand-coding a website is performance. Hand-coded websites are typically leaner and faster because they contain only the necessary code. Site builders, on the other hand, often come with bloated code due to their all-in-one solutions and numerous built-in features, many of which you might not even use. A faster website not only enhances the user experience but also ranks better on search engines, improving your SEO.

## Customisation and Flexibility

Hand-coding gives you complete control over every aspect of your website's design and functionality. You're not limited by the constraints of a template or the features available on a site builder platform. This flexibility means you can create a truly unique website that perfectly aligns with your brand and meets your specific needs. Whether it's a custom feature or a specific design element, hand-coding makes it possible.

## Enhanced Security

Security is a crucial concern for any website. Hand-coded websites are typically more secure than those built with site builders. Site builders are common targets for hackers due to their widespread use and known vulnerabilities. With hand-coding, you have greater control over security measures, ensuring that your website is protected against potential threats.

## SEO Advantages

Hand-coded websites offer better SEO potential. Since you have control over the entire structure and content of your site, you can optimise every element for search engines. This includes proper heading hierarchies, meta tags, alt texts for images, and clean URLs. Site builders might not offer the same level of control or flexibility, potentially limiting your SEO efforts.

## Cost-Effectiveness in the Long Run

While site builders may seem cost-effective initially, especially for small businesses or startups, the long-term benefits of hand-coding can outweigh the initial investment. With hand-coded websites, you're less likely to encounter issues that require costly fixes. Additionally, you won't be paying recurring fees for premium features or plugins, as you might with site builders.
