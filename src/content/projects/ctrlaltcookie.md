## The brief

Cookie needed a single home on the web for a growing personal brand. Fans and collaborators were scattered across half a dozen platforms, and there was no one place that tied it all together or made a strong first impression.

## The challenge

Link-in-bio tools are quick, but they all look the same and none of them felt like Cookie. The site had to capture a distinct personality, work flawlessly on the phones most visitors would arrive on, and stay easy to update as socials and content changed.

## What we built

We designed and hand-coded a bespoke landing page with a bold, playful identity and tasteful animation that rewards interaction without getting in the way. Every social channel, stat and important link is surfaced in one clean, responsive layout, and the whole thing is fast and accessible on any device.

## The outcome

CtrlAltCookie now has a proper front door: a memorable, on-brand hub that turns casual visitors into followers and makes sharing a single link effortless.
