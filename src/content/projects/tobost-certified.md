## The brief

Tobost06 needed a proper home for a growing library of gaming content: news, reviews and updates that were worth reading and easy to keep publishing. The site had to feel credible and organised, not like another cluttered blog.

## The challenge

Content-heavy sites live or die by structure. Articles had to be easy to browse, quick to load and simple to find through search, all while presenting a clean, trustworthy identity that reflected the quality of the writing.

## What we built

We designed and hand-coded a well-structured blog with a clear reading experience and a professional, trustworthy look. News, reviews and updates are organised so readers can navigate easily, and SEO best practices were built in to help the content rank and stay discoverable.

## The outcome

Tobost Certified now has a polished, well-organised platform that gives its gaming content the credible home it deserves and room to keep growing.
